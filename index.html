Demondawn – vocals • Mayhem – guitars • Nick Grave – bass • Messiah Of Evil – drums
 

Skyconqueror is a traditional heavy metal band founded in 1997 in the countryside near Münster. Over the course of the first six years they released three demos - the first full length CD HELLSTORY was released after more than a decade in spring of 2008.

In the following years the band played live and collected material. The second full length CD UNDER THE PENTAGRAM was released in Summer of 2014.

The band has had a stable lineup since 2014. Nick Grave plays bass, Mayhem plays lead and rhythm guitars, lead vocals are performed by Demondawn and drums are tormented by Messiah Of Evil.

SKYCONQUEROR rehearses in Dortmund Nordstadt currently. There they also recorded the THE BLACK RAVEN EP in February of 2023. That release received several positive reviews both live and in the press, including in Rock Hard and Deaf Forever.

 

The release of THE BLACK RAVEN EP also earned them gigs on the German Swordbrothers and the Spirit Of Metal Festival, where they have shared the stage with Atlantean Kodex, Tygers Of Pan Tang, Tytan and Kev Riddles Baphomet to name a few.

Currently the band is working on new songs for the next release - this time aiming for a full length album.

SKYCONQUEROR plays traditional heavy metal inspired by the occult both lyrically and musically.
